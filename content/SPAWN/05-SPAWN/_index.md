## Potential Benefits and Risks 

<!--more-->
Benefits include scalable operations, optimized resource allocation, improved efficiency, and the ability to innovate adaptively in fast-changing markets. Risks involve cascading errors within autonomous chains of decisions, regulatory and ethical challenges, and requirement of new trust frameworks to ensure stakeholder confidence in AI agent decisions.
