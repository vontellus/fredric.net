## Case studies and real-world examples

- Alibaba uses AI extensively across e-commerce, smart city traffic control, and agricultural monitoring, illustrating a large-scale, multifaceted autonomous ecosystem supporting profitability.[https://bernardmarr.com/the-10-best-examples-of-how-companies-use-artificial-intelligence-in-practice/](Bernar Marr)​
    
- DAOs like MakerDAO and Gitcoin show progressive decentralization from traditional companies into autonomous community-operated organizations with significant venture capital involvement, revealing governance tensions and innovation potential.[https://www.belfercenter.org/publication/case-profiles-decentralized-autonomous-organizations](Belfer Center)​
    
- Multi-agent AI systems manage customer service workflows by collaboratively resolving issues, escalating problems, and adjusting policies dynamically.[https://www.sap.com/resources/what-are-multi-agent-systems](SAP)​
    
- Bottom-up emergent change in organizations has successfully empowered employees and improved communication and innovation by moving away from purely hierarchical decision-making.[https://www.acukltd.com/case-studies/category/Emergent+Change](Emergent Change)​
